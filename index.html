About This Site

Welcome to CashAppreciated - the most honest donation site on the internet! This is a joke site, but the donations are very real (and very appreciated).

Here's the deal: I'm a student working on a web development project, and I thought "why not make something fun that could also buy me a coffee?"

No grand causes, no sob stories - just pure honesty and humor. Your donation will directly fuel my caffeine addiction and possibly some late-night coding snacks.
